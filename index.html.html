English Pronouns Practice Questions

Select the type of pronoun. Choose P for personal or Po for possessive pronouns

  1. Does he have a bag with a red tag ?

  2. They got of the bycycles and ran indoors?

  3. This torn dress is not mine ?

  4. Is this luggage theirs ?

Select dp for demonstrative pronouns and da for demonstrative adjectives

  1. look at these beautiful roses

  2. Can you pass me that book with red cover?

  3. We have wached this film sevaral times ?

  4. This is the house where i spent my childhood?

  • Personal pronouns are substitutes for a proper name of a person(he.she,it,you,they etc).
  • Possessive pronouns are all the words that show ownership(my,mine,yours,our etc).
  • Demonstrative pronouns point to specific things(this,that,these etc).
  • Demonstrative adjectives are similar to Demonstrative pronouns but they modify nouns and comes before them.
